=== 3D Model Placement Guide ===

Place GLB/GLTF model files in this directory.

Supported file types: .glb (preferred), .gltf

Model requirements:
  - Poly count: < 5000 (furniture), < 2000 (small devices)
  - Scale: 1 unit = 1 meter
  - Origin: centered at bottom-center of the object
  - Up axis: Y-up
  - Materials: embedded PBR materials (metallic-roughness workflow)

Expected model files:
  bed.glb             — Double bed with headboard (~1.5m wide)
  wardrobe.glb        — Bedroom wardrobe/closet (~0.8m wide)
  sofa.glb            — Living room sofa/sectional (~1.6m wide)
  coffee_table.glb    — Glass/wood coffee table (~0.9m wide)
  tv_stand.glb        — Entertainment center (~1.1m wide)
  dining_table.glb    — Farmhouse dining table (~1.8m long)
  dining_chair.glb    — Wooden dining chair (~0.38m wide)
  bathtub.glb         — Freestanding clawfoot bathtub (~0.7m wide)
  toilet.glb          — Modern one-piece toilet (~0.4m wide)
  sink.glb            — Pedestal or vanity sink (~0.5m wide)
  kitchen_counter.glb — Kitchen counter with cabinets (~4m wide)
  desk.glb            — Office desk (~1.8m wide)
  office_chair.glb    — Ergonomic office chair (~0.4m wide)
  fridge.glb          — Refrigerator (~0.6m wide, ~1.4m tall)
  tv.glb              — Flat screen TV (~0.9m wide)

After adding models:
  1. Host them on a CDN or serve from your backend
  2. Add the URL to app/src/components/house3d/modelRegistry.ts
  3. The 3D viewer will automatically load and use them,
     falling back to procedural geometry if loading fails

Free model sources (look for low-poly, CC-licensed):
  - Sketchfab (https://sketchfab.com — filter by CC license + low poly)
  - Kenney.nl (https://kenney.nl — CC0 furniture packs)
  - Poly Pizza (https://poly.pizza — CC0 models)
  - Quaternius (https://quaternius.com — CC0 packs)
